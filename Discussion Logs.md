## Discussion & Meeting Minutes

### **Meeting 1 – Project Kick-off**

**Week:** 8
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Project idea discussion
* Scope and feasibility
* Task allocation

**Key Discussion Points:**

* Agreed to design a digital training game for grocery store staff
* Chose Scratch as the development platform due to low cost and accessibility
* Decided on 4 game levels based on real grocery store tasks

**Decisions Made:**

* Finalised project theme: Grocery store staff training
* Assigned one level to each team member
* Nafiz to handle final game integration

---

### **Meeting 2 – Business Needs & Innovation**

**Week:** 9
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Identify business needs
* Align game features with training problems

**Key Discussion Points:**

* New staff lack experience and confidence during peak hours
* Traditional training is time-consuming and manpower-heavy
* Digital simulation can provide repeatable and consistent training

**Decisions Made:**

* Mapped each business need to a game level
* Agreed to focus on speed, accuracy, and decision-making

---

### **Meeting 3 – Game Design & Workflow**

**Week:** 10
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Level design and workflows
* Win/lose conditions

**Key Discussion Points:**

* Keep gameplay simple and intuitive
* Avoid complex scoring systems
* Use timers and task completion as main success indicators

**Decisions Made:**

* Each level has one clear objective
* No advanced data tracking or user accounts

---

### **Meeting 4 – Development Progress Check**

**Week:** 11
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Review individual level progress
* Identify issues and risks

**Key Discussion Points:**

* Minor logic issues in early prototypes
* Need to standardise variables and naming

**Decisions Made:**

* Adopt consistent variable naming across all levels
* Continue individual development before integration

---

### **Meeting 5 – Integration & Testing**

**Week:** 13
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Combine levels into one project
* Testing and debugging

**Key Discussion Points:**

* Integration caused minor conflicts in variables

**Decisions Made:**

* Simplified level transitions

---

### **Meeting 6 – Final Review**

**Week:** 15
**Participants:** Sharon, Yu Heng, Nafiz, Rian

**Agenda:**

* Final checks

**Key Discussion Points:**
